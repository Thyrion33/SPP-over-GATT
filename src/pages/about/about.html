Serial GATT Terminal

Hybrid software for Chrome browser, Android and iOS, that allows you to connect to and communicate with the AIRcable MiniMesh.

Utility that connects via BLE and communicates serial data with the AIRcable MiniMesh.

Built by Wireless Cables Inc.

AIRcable MiniMesh

 Serial-port to Bluetooth BLE:
  • SPP-over-GATT: serial port profile over GATT, secured with strong encryption and password.
  • Bluetooth Mesh, when not connected. Transmits RS232 data with encrypted mesh packets.
  • No more range problems, mesh packets are relayed by other mesh nodes.
